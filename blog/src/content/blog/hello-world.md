Welcome to the new blog! This is a sample post to demonstrate the minimalist, SEO-optimized Astro and Tailwind setup.

## The Aesthetic

The design is intentionally stark, focusing purely on content and typography. With absolute black text on a pure white background, readability is maximized.

- **Fast**: Built on Astro, shipping zero client-side JavaScript by default.
- **Beautiful**: Using Tailwind CSS typography plugin for seamless prose styling.
- **Minimal**: No distractions, just your words.
