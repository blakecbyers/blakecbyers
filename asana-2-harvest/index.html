Privacy Policy for Asana Harvest Focus

1. Data Collection

Asana Harvest Focus does not collect, transmit, or sell any user data. The extension operates entirely on your local device.

2. Asana Data

The extension requires your Asana Personal Access Token (PAT) to function.

  • Storage: Your PAT is stored securely in your browser's local storage (chrome.storage / localStorage). It never leaves your device except to communicate directly with the official Asana API.
  • Usage: The extension uses this token solely to fetch your completed tasks for the current day.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Asana: We communicate directly with Asana's API to retrieve your task data. Please refer to Asana's Privacy Policy for how they handle your data.
  • Harvest: The extension generates a text summary for you to paste into Harvest. It does not communicate securely with Harvest's API directly; it links you to their website.

4. Contact
